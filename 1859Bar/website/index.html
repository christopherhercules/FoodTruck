Our Story

Salado's Speakeasy-
Inspired Bar

Named after the year Salado was founded, Bar 1859 is a speakeasy-inspired cocktail lounge rooted in the history and character of one of Texas's most storied communities.

We take pride in our world-class bourbon selection, hand-crafted cocktails, and a food menu that punches well above bar-kitchen expectations. From brisket nachos to Petite Beef Wellington — there's always something worth ordering.

Step outside to our legendary 200+ year old live oak patio — one of the most iconic spots in the Texas Hill Country. Whether it's a quiet bourbon night or a rowdy live music evening, there's a seat waiting for you.

The Patio

Under a 200-Year-
Old Live Oak

Our outdoor patio is shaded by one of the most remarkable trees in Salado — a live oak that's been standing longer than the bar itself. It's the perfect spot for happy hour, live music nights, and warm Texas evenings.
