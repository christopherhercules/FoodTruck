Our Story

Authentic Mexican
Since Day One

At Mas Chingon Mexican Grill, we bring the bold, vibrant flavors of authentic Mexican street food to Salado, Texas. Every dish is crafted fresh with traditional recipes and real ingredients — no shortcuts.

From our sizzling carne asada fajitas to our hand-crafted breakfast tacos served all day, every bite is made with passion and pride.

Dine on our beautiful outdoor patio or grab a quick pickup order. However you enjoy us, we're here to make it mas chingon.
