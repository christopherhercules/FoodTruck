## 1. What the website does

Your website at **livewstn.myserviceflows.com** is a professional lead capture and client communication tool. It does three things:

**a) Finds you apartment-seeking clients.**
Visitors who need to rent chat with your AI assistant, which collects their name, phone, area of interest, budget, bedroom count, move-in date, and any special notes. Once they finish, you get an email (and eventually a text), and the lead appears in your dashboard.

**b) Finds you home buyer clients.**
Same idea — the AI walks home buyers through their needs: location, timeline, whether they are pre-approved, etc.

**c) Finds you home seller clients.**
Sellers enter their address, estimated value, condition of the home, and timeline. You receive the lead immediately.

In addition to the AI chat, there are traditional contact forms for all three services (Rent, Buy, Sell) accessible by scrolling down the page.

## 2. How a customer uses the site

When someone visits your site:

1. They can scroll and read about your services, see testimonials, and fill out a form — **or**
2. They click the **gold chat bubble** in the bottom-right corner and talk to your AI assistant named "Brogan's AI assistant."

**The AI chat flow for a renter looks like this:**

- Customer clicks "Find an Apartment"
- AI asks: What area of Texas? (e.g. San Antonio, North Side)
- AI asks: What's your monthly budget?
- AI asks: How many bedrooms? (Studio / 1 bed / 2 bed / 3 bed / 3+)
- AI asks: What's your target move-in date?
- AI asks: Anything I should know upfront? (pets, credit, broken lease — reassures them it's okay)
- AI asks: Your name?
- AI asks: Best phone number for Brogan to reach you?
- AI closes with: "Brogan will contact you within the hour."
- **Bonus:** If you have added properties to your dashboard that match their budget and bedroom count, the AI will automatically show them up to 3 recommended properties right in the chat.

**The AI chat flow for a home buyer:**
Area, budget, timeline, pre-approval status, move-in timeline, name, phone.

**The AI chat flow for a home seller:**
Property address, estimated asking price, condition of the home, timeline, name, phone.

The AI also handles common questions without escalating (e.g., "How much does this cost?" → "Nothing. Zero. Free for renters. Properties pay a referral fee.")

## 4. Your dashboard — complete walkthrough

Go to: **https://livewstn.myserviceflows.com/dashboard.html**

When prompted, enter the password: **`WSTN2025!`**

The dashboard has three tabs:

---

### Tab 1: Leads

This is your main working view. All leads are listed here, newest first.

**At the top:** Summary cards showing total leads, how many are new, how many are active, and how many are closed this week.

**The lead table** shows each person's name, type (Rent/Buy/Sell), phone, status, and when they submitted. Click any row to open their full detail panel on the right side.

**In the detail panel you can:**
- See everything they submitted in the chat
- Change their **Status** from the dropdown:
  - `New` — just came in
  - `Called` — you have reached out
  - `Showing` — actively showing them properties
  - `Active` — in progress
  - `Closed` — deal done
  - `Lost` — not moving forward
- Add **Internal Notes** (only you can see these — clients never see them)
- Click the phone number to call them directly

Changes save automatically when you click outside the field or change the status dropdown.

---

### Tab 2: Properties

This is where you manage the properties you recommend to clients. **This is the cross-device feature** — properties you add here will automatically show up in the AI chat for renters who match the budget and bedroom count. It works from any device, not just the one you added them on.

**To add a property:**
1. Click **"Add a Property"**
2. Fill in: Property/Community name (required), Type, Street address, City, Min price, Max price
3. Check the bedroom sizes available (Studio, 1 Bedroom, 2 Bedrooms, etc.)
4. Optionally add Amenities (e.g., "Pool, gym, pet-friendly")
5. Optionally add Notes — **these are private and never shown to clients** (e.g., "Manager is Maria, call 210-555-0101 for fastest response")
6. Make sure "Active" is checked
7. Click **Save Property**

**How matching works in the chat:**
After a renter finishes the chat, the AI checks your active properties. If a property's price range overlaps with the client's budget AND the property offers the bedroom size they need, it gets suggested. Up to 3 properties are shown.

**To temporarily hide a property** without deleting it: click Edit → uncheck "Active" → Save. It will stay in your dashboard but won't show in the chat.

---

### Tab 3: Social Poster

This lets you post to your Facebook Page and Instagram Business account at the same time, directly from the dashboard, with AI-generated captions.

**How it works:**
1. Upload a photo (drag and drop or click to select — also works from your phone camera)
2. The AI analyzes the photo and writes **3 ready-to-post caption options** in your brand voice (real estate / apartment locating)
3. Click the caption you like (you can edit it before posting)
4. Click **"Post to Facebook & Instagram"**
5. The post goes live on both platforms simultaneously

> **Note:** This feature requires a one-time Facebook and Instagram setup. See Section 7 below.
